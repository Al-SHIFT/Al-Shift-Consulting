Solutions That Shift

Turning Ideas Into Learning Journeys

At AL-SHIFT, we offer a flexible portfolio of services designed to meet the evolving needs of our partners across sectors. Whether you're a global institution, a grassroots initiative, or something in between—we work with you to co-create learning experiences that are contextual, strategic, and transformative.

Programme Planning, Design and Management

We design responsive, goal-driven programmes tailored to diverse educational, cultural, and development contexts—from initial concept to implementation planning.

  • Programme strategy development
  • Educational frameworks and curricula
  • Implementation planning
  • Project management and coordination

Capacity Building & Training

We build the skills of individuals and teams through engaging workshops, training of trainers (ToT), coaching, and hybrid learning models—both digital and in-person.

  • Workshop facilitation and design
  • Training of trainers (ToT)
  • Coaching and mentoring
  • Hybrid learning experiences

Digital & Instructional Design

We create inclusive, tech-enabled learning tools and curricula that are user-centred, interactive, and grounded in real-world application.

  • Instructional design for various platforms
  • Interactive learning modules
  • Digital learning experiences
  • Multimedia content creation

Research, Content Development and R&D

We lead and support research, needs assessments, and content creation to inform policy, strengthen practice, and generate knowledge that drives learning innovation.

  • Needs assessments and context analysis
  • Learning research
  • Content development
  • Innovation research and development

PMEL (Planning, Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning)

We integrate agile MEL strategies to track progress, capture learning, and inform adaptive decision-making across the project lifecycle.

  • Monitoring and evaluation frameworks
  • Impact assessment
  • Learning documentation
  • Adaptive management support

AI for Learning & Innovation

We help organizations harness the power of artificial intelligence to enhance learning experiences, streamline processes, and support innovation. From using AI for content creation, instructional design, and digital storytelling, to applying it in PMEL and adaptive learning systems—we integrate AI tools ethically and intentionally to make learning more responsive, personalized, and impactful.

  • AI integration in learning design
  • Personalized learning systems
  • Ethical AI implementation
  • AI for content creation and curation
