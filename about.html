AL-SHIFT Consulting: Agile Learning Solutions

AL-SHIFT Consulting was founded on the belief that meaningful learning can drive lasting transformation—for individuals, organizations, and communities.

With our team's international experience in education, programme design, capacity building, and digital innovation, AL-SHIFT brings deep expertise and a human-centred approach to every project. Our work is grounded in agile thinking and driven by a commitment to inclusion, creativity, and impact.

We specialize in designing advanced and customized learning experiences that respond to the unique needs of our partners. Whether it's a grassroots initiative or a global strategy, we co-create solutions that are adaptive, culturally relevant, and built for real-world change.

Our Vision

Learning is not just content, and more than a tool. It's a strategy for transformation—crafted to adapt, empower, and drive change that's lasting, inclusive, and grounded in context.

Our Mission

To shift learning from static to strategic—co-creating agile, inclusive experiences that empower people and organizations to grow, adapt, and drive meaningful change.

Our Values

Inclusion & Equity

We centre diverse voices and design learning that is accessible, contextual, and responsive to different lived experiences.

Agility

We embrace change and complexity—adapting strategies and solutions to meet evolving needs with flexibility and creativity.

Collaboration

We co-create with our partners, building on trust, dialogue, and shared ownership at every stage of the process.

Innovation

We integrate cutting-edge tools, methodologies, and technologies to shape forward-thinking, future-ready learning experiences.

Cultural Sensitivity

We work with deep respect for cultural contexts, traditions, and identities—ensuring our approaches are locally grounded and globally aware.

Human-Centred Impact

We focus on real people, real needs, and real results—ensuring that impact is not only measured, but genuinely felt.

The Shift Behind AL-SHIFT

"Learning is not just content—it's a strategy for transformation."

I'm a learning strategist, facilitator, and programme designer with over 15 years of international experience working at the intersection of education, human rights, youth empowerment, and innovation.

AL-SHIFT Consulting was born from years of working alongside communities, educators, and changemakers across diverse regions and realities. Over time, I saw a clear need: for learning that is not only innovative, but also inclusive, agile, and deeply rooted in context.

Too often, learning solutions are designed in isolation from the people they aim to serve. AL-SHIFT is my response to that, an invitation to do things differently. To co-create experiences that adapt to complexity, honour lived experiences, and drive meaningful, lasting transformation.

This work is more than a profession, it's a purpose. And it's built on the belief that learning, when done right, can be one of the most powerful tools for equity, empowerment, and change.

Before launching AL-SHIFT, I held leadership roles with local, regional and international organisations. I've led initiatives in STEAM education, life skills, social innovation, and Human Rights across the MENA region, especially in complex or fragile environments.

I'm passionate about designing learning experiences that are not only innovative but also deeply human. I've developed curricula, facilitated trainings, and managed programmes, all rooted in a belief that learning, when done right, can be one of the most powerful tools for transformation.

With degrees from the University of Amsterdam, University College London, and Birzeit University, and a background in education, international development, architecture and museum studies, I bring an interdisciplinary lens to every project I work on.

Shirin Husseini

Founder, Learning Architect & Lead Consultant
